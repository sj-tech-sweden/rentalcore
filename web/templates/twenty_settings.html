How It Works

Customers

Customers with a company name are synced as Companies in Twenty. Individual customers (first/last name only) are synced as People.

Jobs

Jobs are synced as Opportunities in Twenty. The job status is mapped to a Twenty pipeline stage and the revenue is stored as the opportunity amount.

Two-way customer sync

Customer changes made in Twenty are synced back to RentalCore via webhook. Only customers originally pushed from RentalCore are affected. Job opportunities remain one-way (RentalCore → Twenty).
