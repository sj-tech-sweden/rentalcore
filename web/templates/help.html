Getting started

Start with these essentials to configure RentalCore for your organisation.

  • Company setup: review branding, legal info and default templates in Settings → Company.
  • Users & roles: grant access via System → Security → Roles and assign least privilege.
  • Inventory import: bulk-load devices and cases from CSV using the Bulk Operations tool.

Daily workflows

Follow these flows to keep jobs, devices and cables in sync.

  • Create a job: Jobs → New, assign customer, schedule dates and select devices.
  • Track device readiness: update pack status from the job detail modal or scanner board.
  • Cable lookup: filter by plug type, length and cross-section in the enhanced cable view.

Troubleshooting

Resolve common issues with these quick checks.

  • Missing device: verify it isn’t assigned to an overlapping job via Devices → Availability.
  • Scanner offline: restart the dedicated service with ./restart-dev.sh on the host.
  • Export fails: confirm the archive directory is writable and retry the export from Analytics.
